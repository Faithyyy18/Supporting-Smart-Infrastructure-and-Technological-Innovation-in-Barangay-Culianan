Issues We Support

The "Supporting Smart Infrastructure and Technological Innovation in Barangay Culianan" advocacy addresses several critical issues to ensure sustainable growth and improve the quality of life for residents. It focuses on modernizing infrastructure by integrating smart technologies into urban planning, transportation, and public utilities to enhance efficiency and accessibility. The advocacy works to bridge the digital divide by promoting digital literacy and providing technology access through community Wi-Fi zones and learning hubs. It emphasizes sustainable energy solutions, including renewable energy adoption and smart energy management, to reduce carbon emissions. Environmental sustainability is also a priority, with initiatives for intelligent waste management, recycling, and eco-friendly practices. Public safety is enhanced through smart surveillance systems and data-driven emergency response technologies, while economic empowerment is supported by equipping local businesses with digital tools and creating technology-driven job opportunities. Additionally, the advocacy prioritizes disaster preparedness with real-time monitoring and resilient infrastructure to mitigate risks. Together, these efforts aim to build a progressive, sustainable, and technologically empowered Barangay Culianan.
